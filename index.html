    About the Game

    This app helps you explore Stardew Valley NPC preferences. Select a character to see what they love, like, dislike, or hate. Great for building friendships in-game!

    How to use

    Click on any NPC card or search by name to view their gift preferences. Use the tag shortcuts to jump to specific categories like Loved or Hated gifts.
